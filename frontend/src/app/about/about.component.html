💈 About Elite Barber Demo

Welcome to Elite Barber Demo, the ultimate barbershop scheduling app designed to showcase a seamless and efficient way to book your next haircut.

Whether you're looking for a classic cut, a modern fade, or a beard trim, our demo platform demonstrates how easy and intuitive appointment scheduling can be.

🗓 Features

  • ✅ Select a preferred date
  • ✅ View available time slots
  • ✅ Instantly book an appointment

Our demo application is built with Django & Angular, ensuring a fast, responsive, and user-friendly experience. Try it out and see how effortless booking a barber appointment can be!
